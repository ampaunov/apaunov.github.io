I'm Alex Paunov.

I write here about the things I happen to be thinking about — sometimes philosophy, sometimes method, sometimes the small craft questions that come up in modeling and analysis. The posts are written for myself first, in the sense that I find clear writing easier than clear thinking, and the cheapest path to one is often the other.

The site is deliberately simple: text, the occasional image, a few references. There is no algorithm here. The thread connecting one post to the next is whatever was on my mind that week.

If you want to write back, my email is at the top of the page (eventually).
